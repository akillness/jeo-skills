# Triage
- Problem: `technical-writing` is a legacy catch-all skill that overlaps with `user-guide-writing`, `api-documentation`, and `changelog-maintenance`, lacks support files and evals, and exceeds the recommended SKILL.md length.
- Audience: Maintainers and agent users who need a reliable internal technical-docs anchor for specs, architecture docs, ADRs, runbooks, migration notes, and incident-facing documentation.
- Why now: Graphify now flags the documentation cluster as a duplicate-prone area, and `technical-writing` is one of the remaining widely indexed legacy anchors still missing both `references/` and `evals/`.
