# Lane B — Solutions

The nearby landscape does not point to a missing wrapper. It points to a recurring split:
- operator-facing runtimes such as ClawTeam
- coding-agent shells like OpenHands
- generic orchestration frameworks like CrewAI, AutoGen, and OpenAI Swarm
- tmux/worktree operator kits like claude-swarm, wt, Legio, and Goblin Forge

Across those categories, repeated complaints center on reliability, observability, runtime friction, and trust boundaries — not on a lack of another orchestration abstraction.
