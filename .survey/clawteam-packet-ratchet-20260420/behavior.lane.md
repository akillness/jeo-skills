# Lane C — Actual Behavior

The most durable behavior finding is that ClawTeam-style workflows are still fundamentally tmux + git-worktree + manual operator systems. Users attach to sessions, inspect panes, inject loop instructions, script watchdogs, prune worktrees, and work around trust prompts or config gaps. That makes a packet-first, reality-first `clawteam` front door more useful than a larger abstraction-heavy skill.
