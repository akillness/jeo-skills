# Triage
- Problem: `sprint-retrospective` is still a legacy template-heavy skill and lags the rest of the modernized PM cluster on trigger clarity, facilitation modes, action follow-through, and support files.
- Audience: engineering managers, scrum masters, product managers, tech leads, and game-production leads running sprint, milestone, or release retrospectives.
- Why now: `task-planning` and `standup-meeting` were already modernized, so the remaining PM gap is the reflection/follow-through lane rather than adding another overlapping planning skill.
