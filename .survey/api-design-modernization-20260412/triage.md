# Triage
- Problem: `api-design` is a broad legacy skill with no support files, and it overlaps with `api-documentation` around OpenAPI/spec authoring instead of owning a cleaner contract-first boundary.
- Audience: Agents and users doing backend/API planning, contract design, review, versioning, auth/error/pagination decisions, and handoffs to implementation/testing/documentation.
- Why now: The backend cluster still has several older high-traffic skills without references/evals, and `api-design` is one of the clearest remaining candidates for a bounded upgrade that improves trigger quality and reduces overlap without adding a noisy duplicate.
