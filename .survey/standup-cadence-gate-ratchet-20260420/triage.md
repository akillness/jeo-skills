# Triage
- Problem: `standup-meeting` still assumes a daily ritual too early; it needs an upfront cadence gate so the skill can recommend live-daily, async-daily, hybrid-daily, less-frequent syncs, or no recurring standup when daily coordination is low-signal.
- Audience: Software/dev teams, product and ops teams, marketing/GTM launch teams, and game-delivery teams deciding whether a daily standup is justified.
- Why now: The PM cluster is already routing-first and structurally stable, so the best bounded improvement is tightening `standup-meeting` around cadence truth instead of adding another ceremony or async-checkin wrapper.
