# Triage
- Problem: Small game teams have playtest notes, demo reactions, creator feedback, wishlist/context signals, and technical findings, but no reusable skill that turns that mixed packet into a prioritized fix brief.
- Audience: Indie developers, solo devs, small studios, publisher helpers, and agent-assisted game teams shipping demos or Steam launches.
- Why now: The repo already added build-log, performance, and Steam launch skills, but still lacks the post-playtest synthesis layer that tells teams what to fix first before the next playtest, festival, or release beat.
