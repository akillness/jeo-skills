# Triage
- Problem: Teams need a reusable workflow for turning vague Unity/Unreal performance complaints into a profiling plan, likely bottleneck classification, and next optimization artifact instead of generic "optimize performance" advice.
- Audience: Indie developers, technical designers, gameplay programmers, and small studios shipping Unity or Unreal projects on PC, Steam Deck, consoles, and mobile.
- Why now: The repository already has game log triage and Steam launch ops skills, but no game-engine performance triage/profiling skill. This is a recurring, high-leverage pain area that frequently falls back to ad hoc spreadsheets, profiler screenshots, and generic optimization checklists.
