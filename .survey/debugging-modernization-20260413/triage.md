# Triage
- Problem: Modernize the legacy `debugging` skill into a clearer, higher-utility systematic debugging workflow with stronger boundaries versus `log-analysis`, `testing-strategies`, and `code-review`.
- Audience: AI agents and developers debugging runtime failures, regressions, flaky behavior, and reproduction/isolation problems across CLI, backend, frontend, and fullstack work.
- Why now: `debugging` is a high-utility legacy skill that still lacks support files and explicit handoff rules, while nearby skills have recently been modernized. Tightening it should improve activation quality and reduce overlap across the code-quality cluster.
