Use this as the default skeleton after choosing a mode.

```markdown

## Scope
- Domain:
- Stage:
- Strongest unknown:
- Downstream target:
- Confidence:

## Chosen mode
- problem-framing | audience-and-value-framing | concept-shaping | game-concept-framing | story-packaging

## What matters most now
- Bullet 1
- Bullet 2
- Bullet 3

## Recommended artifact
- problem brief | positioning brief | concept brief | game concept packet | story / pitch packet

## Artifact contents
| Section | Decision | Why it matters now |
|---------|----------|--------------------|
| Problem / audience / fantasy | ... | ... |
| Value / hook / transformation | ... | ... |
| Constraints / scope / evidence | ... | ... |
| Open questions | ... | ... |

## Immediate next steps
1. ...
2. ...
3. ...

## Route-outs
- Skill / workflow:
- Why next:
- What to pass forward:

## What not to do yet
- ...
- ...
```

## Mode-specific emphasis
- **Problem framing** → bias toward pain, stakes, and assumptions
- **Audience and value framing** → bias toward user, JTBD, differentiation, and proof
- **Concept shaping** → bias toward the shortest crisp summary plus scope constraints
- **Game concept framing** → bias toward fantasy, pillars, loop, and first-playable proof
- **Story packaging** → bias toward narrative tension, promise, proof, and ask
