# Asset and QA checklist

## Minimum asset packet
| Asset | Why it matters |
|------|-----------------|
| Brand-safe logo / font / color guidance | Prevents template drift across variants |
| Source footage, screenshots, or script | Defines what can actually be rendered |
| Aspect-ratio targets | Social and ad variants change layout and safe areas |
| Caption / transcript source | Caption work is one of the most failure-prone last-mile steps |
| CTA / destination URL or next action | Prevents a polished video with no clear outcome |

## QA checklist
- Hook and first 2-3 seconds still make sense without audio
- Captions fit safe areas and line breaks do not destroy pacing
- 9:16, 1:1, and 16:9 variants do not crop the wrong focal content
- Brand assets match the current campaign or product state
- Audio levels, music ducking, and narration timing are acceptable
- Final file naming, metadata, and publish handoff are explicit

## Common misses
- Treating captions as an afterthought
- Forgetting aspect-ratio-specific layout checks
- Assuming personalized data fields are always populated
- Rendering drafts without deciding who approves the final output
